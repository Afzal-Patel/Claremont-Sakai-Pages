Gradebook
Gradebook is a tool that can be added to your course site in order to record students' scores and feedback.

    Features

  • To see how are grades calculated and how to manage your site's gradebook, see grade calculation and gradebook management.

  • Note: Items in Gradebook with the tool icon in the column header and grayed-out padlock icons in the cells are being sent to the Gradebook from other tools such as Assignments or Test & Quizzes. Although you can view them, you cannot edit those grades directly from the Gradebook tool.
