File and Folder Naming Conventions
If you use special characters (or any computer reserved characters) in a file or folder name, your site participants may not be able to access the file properly.
  • Whenever you create a new folder or file, follow these naming conventions to ensure that your folders and files work within Sakai and your course participants have proper access.

×

Recommended Characters

  1. Letters: A-Z (either lowercase or uppercase)

  2. Numbers: 0-9

  3. Hyphen: " - "

  4. Underscore: " _ "

×

Unsupported Characters

  1. Less Than: " < "
  2. Greater Than: " > "
  3. Equal: " = "
  4. Semicolon: " ; "
  5. Colon: " : "
  6. Double Quotation: " "
  7. Apostrophe: '
  8. Period: " . "
  9. Question Mark: " ? "
  10. Asterisk: " * "
  11. Carat: " ^ "
  12. Hash Mark: " # "
  13. At Symbol: " @ "
  14. Ampersand: " & "
  15. Percent: " % "
  16. Forward Slash: " / "
  17. Backlash: " \ "
  18. Vertical Bar or Pipe: " | "
