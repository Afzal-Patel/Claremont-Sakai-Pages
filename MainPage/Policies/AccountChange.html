Account Change Service


There are two types of accounts in Claremont Sakai: registered accounts and guest accounts.

  • Updating Account Information


      Registered Users

    • Registered users cannot update their registered accounts since this information is tied to the school account. A registered user's information in Sakai can be updated only by request when it is changed legally and/or the registrar information has been updated.

      • Guest Users
    • You can update your guest account as follows:

      • 1. Login to Sakai with your guest account.

      • 2. Click on Account on the left menu bar.

      • 3. Click on Modify Details button.

      • 4. Update your account information and click on Update Details for saving your update.



      • 5. If you cannot login, reset your Guest Account password here: Guest Password Reset.

    *If you have any questions or would like more information regarding the Claremont Sakai policies, please contact your school's IT department.
