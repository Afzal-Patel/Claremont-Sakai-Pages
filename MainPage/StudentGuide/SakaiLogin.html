Sakai Login
Claremont Sakai provides "single sign-on" capabilities which allows users to access any web services that uses CAS by signing in once with campus credentials.

    Claremont Students

  • CGU, CMC, CUC, HMC, JSD, KGI, PZR, and SCR students:

        To login, click the top-right "Login" button and it will redirect you to the Claremont Authentication System (CAS). Next, enter your Sakai username (your username plus an '@' followed by your institution's three-letter code. You may also select the name of your college from the "select your college" drop-down in CAS, and then enter your college login id and password. If you select "Remember My College", you will not need to select your college on subsequent logins.

    POM students:

        You will be prompted to finish login through your Pomona microsoft email account. CAS for Pomona students was updated during the summer of 2020.


    Notes    : Your school may require two-factor authentication for CAS. For more information about account information see Updating Account Information.


    • Guest Users

  • Guest accounts can be created for non-Claremont College students. If a guest account has been created for you, use your email address as your login name. Sakai will send you a temporary password in an e-mail message.

  • 1. Click the top-right button for "Guest Login"

  • 2. Enter your email address and temporary password

  • 3. Once you login you should change your password via "Home" (tab along the top of the Sakai page), "Account" (tool in the Menu Bar at right), "Modify Details" (button and enter a new password), and "Update Details" (button to save your new password). For help updating guest account information see Updating Guest Information.

    • Assistance

    1. To reset your Guest Account password use: Guest Password Reset.

    2. If you need to change your Guest Account information, please submit a request to your college's IT representative using the Account Change Request Template

    3. If you need help with Sakai, please contact your school's IT department.

    4. For more information about the Sakai Policies at the Claremont Colleges, please see the Claremont College Sakai Policies page.

    5. By logging into this system, you are agreeing to abide by the terms set forth by your College's Appropriate Use document. Please contact your school's IT department if you do not have a copy of this document.
